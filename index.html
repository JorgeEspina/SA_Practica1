Naan Bread

A delicious yeasty Indian bread perfect for scooping up delicious curries. You can also eat it plain with butter or ghee!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour or 1/2 all-purpose 1/2 whole wheat
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water, hot

Directions: Mix hot water, sugar and yeast and let stand for 10 mins or until froathy. Then add baking powder, baking soda, salt, milk and flour(a little at a time). Let stand for an hour. Then seperate into 12-15 small balls and let stand for another 30 minutes. After that roll them flat and bake at 500 degrees for 2-3 minutes on each side.

German Pancakes

German pancakes are super yummy and super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 6 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

Directions:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Melt butter ina medium baking dish. In a medium bowl, mix flour, milk, eggs and salt. pour the mixture into a prepared baking dish. Bake on center rack in the preheated oven for 30 - 40 minutes, until golden brown.

French Toast

French toast is easy and delicious breakfast when you are in a hurry.

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 slices of french bread
  • 1/4 tablespoon butter

Directions: In a small bowl, combine, cinnamon, vanilla, milk, eggs.In a 10 inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk together cinnamon, vanilla, milk, eggs and pour in to a shallow container. Dip bread in egg mixture. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook other side. Serve with syrup
